History OF Liverpool chips

Located 30 minutes from one of New York's major cities, Liverpool Chips is founded on the pride and dedication of Syracuse, NY. Founded in 2023 by a student for a project for CSCI 3703 Interface Design and Development, this chip company puts pride in each product.
